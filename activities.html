Fun Things To Do

Nelson Mandela Square

Nelson Mandela Square, located in the heart of Sandton, offers a perfect blend of shopping and entertainment, making it a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a great time. The square is lined with high-end boutiques, designer stores, and unique shops, catering to fashion lovers and those seeking exclusive items. After a shopping spree, visitors can relax at one of the many stylish restaurants or cafes, enjoying a meal or drink while soaking in the lively atmosphere. With its vibrant vibe, stunning statue of Nelson Mandela, and a variety of activities, it’s the ideal spot for a fun day out with friends or family.

Sun City Resort

Just 2-hours drive from Johannesburg, Sun City is the perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of the city. This luxurious resort offers beautiful hotels, world-class restaurants, and a range of activities. Whether you’re hitting the beach at the Valley of Waves, teeing off at the famous Gary Player Golf Course, or indulging in a spa treatment, there's something for everyone. It's ideal for a quick weekend break or a longer vacation. And if you’re into wildlife, it's close to the Pilanesberg National Park for safaris and game drives, bringing that wild, nature-filled experience right to your doorstep. It’s also a great way to unwind with friends, a romantic retreat, or a fun-filled family holiday. Sun City is truly a hidden gem in our own backyard!

Walter Sisulu National Botanical Garden

The Walter Sisulu National Botanical Garden is located in the western part of Johannesburg, South Africa, specifically in Roodepoort. This garden is one of the largest of its kind in the country and is known for its rich biodiversity and stunning natural beauty. It was named in honor of Walter Sisulu, a prominent anti-apartheid activist and a member of the African National Congress (ANC), who played a key role in the struggle for South Africa's liberation. The garden features a wide variety of South Africa’s indigenous plant species, including fynbos, savanna, a waterfall and several walking trails. The garden is an ideal spot for nature lovers, romantic picnics, family outings and those seeking tranquility within Johannesburg’s urban landscape.

Sabi Sands Game Reserve

The most famous private game reserve of all. Sabi Sands, adjacent to South Africa's flagship Kruger National Park, offers a safari experience found nowhere else in the world. Game viewing possibilities are simply unparalleled, including the much sought after 'Big 5'. Add a fantastic range of luxury lodges suiting all tastes and an African dream safari is born. There are no fences between Kruger National Park and Sabi Sands, so wildlife roams freely in a huge natural environment. The Game Reserve is private, meaning that it is not accessible for day visitors. No self-catering, camping, or picnic facilities are available on site. As such, you need a booking at one of the Sabi Sands lodges upon entering the reserve.

Montecasino

Montecasino, located in Fourways, Johannesburg, is much more than just a casino—it's a complete entertainment complex and one of South Africa's top attractions. Montecasino offers a perfect blend of entertainment, dining, shopping, and relaxation. With its stunning Tuscan-inspired architecture, the complex boasts a world-class casino, over 30 restaurants and bars, a vibrant theatre for live shows, and a luxurious cinema. Whether you’re seeking exciting gaming, indulging in gourmet meals, or enjoying family-friendly activities like the Birds of Prey Centre and bowling, Montecasino has something for everyone. Located conveniently near major attractions, it’s the ideal spot to immerse yourself in Johannesburg's dynamic culture while enjoying top-tier entertainment.
