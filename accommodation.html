The Best Accommodation in Johannesburg

The Houghton Hotel

The Houghton Hotel was voted Africa’s best five-star hotel, is central to The Houghton experience. It combines luxury hotel living with a private club, designed to cater for your every need. This gorgeous establishment serves as the premier destination for travelers who seek luxury, convenience and splendid service. From outstanding hospitality and luxury amenities to a desired location in the heart Johannesburg, we’re the perfect destination for both business and leisure visits. Within this stunning precinct, residents and visitors can enjoy the championship golf course, Fitbar gym, four stunning pools, a sauna and steam area, gourmet restaurants and a very exclusive spa.

Four Seasons Hotel The Westcliff

Four Seasons Hotel, located in the prestigious suburb of Westcliff in Johannesburg, is an iconic luxury hotel that offers an extraordinary blend of sophistication, comfort, and breathtaking views. Perched on the edge of the Westcliff Ridge, the hotel is set amidst lush, landscaped gardens, offering panoramic vistas of the city and the surrounding natural beauty. The hotel is situated on a hilltop overlooking the city of Johannesburg and is spread over 13 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens. The hotel offers a range of luxurious amenities and services to its guests, including a full-service spa, outdoor swimming pool, fitness center, and multiple dining options. The Four Seasons is a perfect sanctuary for both relaxation and exploration, with the city’s vibrant attractions just a short distance away.

Fairlawns Boutique Hotel and Spa

Fairlawns Boutique Hotel, located in the serene suburb of Morningside, Sandton, is a distinguished haven for those seeking luxury, tranquility, and personalized service. This five-star boutique hotel is set within a magnificent property, a stately mansion dating back to the 1920s. The grounds feature manicured gardens, tranquil courtyards, and inviting outdoor spaces, creating an oasis perfect for relaxation. The renowned Fairlawns Restaurant delivers exceptional,seasonal cuisines, with menus inspired by local and international flavors. Perfectly blending heritage, luxury, and convenience, Fairlawns is a sanctuary for those seeking an exquisite escape from the demands of city life.

The Marriott Hotel Melrose Arch

The Marriott is a sophisticated and contemporary hotel located in the vibrant Melrose Arch precinct. Known for its upscale design and exceptional service, this five-star hotel offers an ideal blend of modern luxury and convenience. Situated in one of the city’s most trendy and sought-after areas, it is surrounded by a dynamic mix of restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues, making it a perfect destination for business and leisure travelers. The in-house restaurant provides a delicious range of international and local dishes, focusing on fresh, seasonal ingredients. The Marriott offers a luxurious and memorable experience for guests looking to enjoy the best of Johannesburg.

Palazzo Hotel Montecasino

The Palazzo Hotel at Montecasino is a luxurious, five-star property nestled in the heart of the Montecasino entertainment complex in Fourways. Renowned for its Mediterranean-inspired architecture and exceptional service, the Palazzo provides an opulent retreat for both leisure and business travelers. Guests can indulge in relaxation at the luxurious spa, which offers a range of rejuvenating treatments, or take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool set amidst lush gardens. The Palazzo at Montecasino is the perfect destination for an unforgettable stay in Johannesburg.
