THE BEST THINGS TO DO

NELSON MANDELA SQUARE

This award-winning shopping centre, adjoined to Sandton City is located in the heart of South Africa’s financial district, and the very place that made this district so highly sought after. Home to over 300 of the world’s most in-demand brands Sandton City is a shopping centre tailored to the preferences of many. From an eco-fashionable Food District, a family-focussed Fun District, to a chandelier draped Diamond Walk which is home to many global luxury brands. Each year Sandton City is frequented by millions of locals and tourists alike, those with a flare to experience the best of South African retail.

HAVE AN AUTHENTIC JO'BURG EXPERIENCE

MABONENG PRECINCT

Once labelled one of the coolest neighbourhoods in the world (according to Forbes magazine), Maboneng was a golden child among Joburg’s regeneration stories after it was transformed from an industrial and factory district to a buzzing lifestyle playground. The development of the Maboneng Precinct began in 2008 and now, this colourful and youthful neighbourhood has cemented its place as a popular Joburg visitors attraction. Maboneng's main high street is Fox Street, home to the pioneer development Arts on Main and an eclectic selection of dozens of bars, cafe,s and restaurants. Maboneng has now become a major nightlife hotspot and on Saturdays the streets are thronged with a young party crowd.
